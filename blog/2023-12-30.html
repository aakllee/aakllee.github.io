2023 in Review▮▮▮▮

2023-12-30

The year was 2023. It began—as all years do—in January.

For the first half of the year I was mostly absorbed in revision for A-levels, getting my coursework done, etc. so all I can say is I'm glad all that work paid off in the end; got A*A*AA, became the first person in the history of the school to achieve 100% on their CS NEA, got into Warwick, all excellent results.

After all that you'd expect smooth sailing, and you'd be right—so far the course has been mostly content I've already covered before, so I've been studying systems biology on the side for a while now. Not the most relaxing way to spend my Winter break, but it's sure been fun at least! I'm so glad I finally have access to a library.

I've not completed much with regards to any personal projects of mine unfortunately, but such is life... I'd quite like to do something in bioinformatics but I haven't thought of anything just yet. But I digress.

2023! It's been quite the wonderful year in the world what? 8 billion people and all.
